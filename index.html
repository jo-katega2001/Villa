Travel Planning

Experience seamless travel planning with our expert team. We can assist you in arranging transportation and creating custom itineraries to explore the beautiful sights of Nungwi, Zanzibar.

Catering Service

Indulge in delicious meals prepared by our experienced chefs. We offer a wide range of culinary delights, including local Zanzibari cuisine and international dishes, to satisfy your taste buds.

Babysitting

Enjoy a worry-free stay at Terry's Villa with our professional babysitting service. Our trained staff will ensure that your children are well taken care of, allowing you to relax and unwind.

Laundry

Leave your laundry responsibilities to us. Our efficient laundry service will take care of your garments, ensuring that you have fresh and clean clothes throughout your stay at Terry's Villa.

Hire a Driver

Sit back and relax while exploring Nungwi with our reliable driver service. Whether you need transportation for sightseeing or airport transfers, our skilled drivers will ensure a comfortable journey.

Bar & Drinks

Unwind and enjoy refreshing beverages at our bar. Choose from a variety of cocktails, mocktails, and other thirst-quenching drinks as you soak up the tropical vibes of Nungwi, Zanzibar.
